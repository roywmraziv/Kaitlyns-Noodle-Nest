About Us

Based in Bolingbrook, Illinois, Kaitlyn's Noodle Nest is a family-owned restaurant. The restaurant serves to bring the taste of yummy homemade noodles to the public.

The inspiration for the restaurant comes from Kaitlyn's signature Macaroni recipe with gooey cheese and the noodles cooked to perfection.

The restaurant has a home-like vibe and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Kaitlyn's Noodle Nest is owned by Kaitlyn, the namesake, and her husband Roy. It is Kaitlyn's recipes that make the restaurant special.

To craft the menu, Kaitlyn relies on her famous recipes and her experience with preparing food for her children. She works to provide yummy food with only the best ingredients that remain nutritious and healthy.

Roy does the marketing and management of the finances and website. He works to ensure everything behind the scenes runs smoothly so Kaitlyn can focus on her passion to bring the best noodles straight to your table.
